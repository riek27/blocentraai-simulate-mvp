Learning from Addis Ababa

The transformation of Addis Ababa through strategic infrastructure development has captured the attention of leaders across Africa...

The transformation of Addis Ababa through strategic infrastructure development has captured the attention of leaders across Africa, including South Sudan's Minister of Land, Housing, and Urban Development, Michael Chanjiek. Speaking during the inaugural Africa Urban Forum held in Addis Ababa from September 4-6, 2024, Minister Chanjiek emphasized the importance of learning from Ethiopia's capital to inform South Sudan's own urban development strategies.

Addis Ababa's rapid transformation offers valuable lessons in urban planning, infrastructure development, and sustainable growth that can be adapted to South Sudan's unique context as the nation works to rebuild and develop its cities.
