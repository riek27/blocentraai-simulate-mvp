Frequently Asked Questions

Is BlocentraAI custodial?

No, BlocentraAI is completely non-custodial. We never hold your Bitcoin or private keys. Our technology enables liquidity activation through smart contracts and cross-chain bridges while you maintain full control of your assets.

How do you 'unlock' idle BTC safely?

Our AI engine analyzes on-chain patterns to identify optimal liquidity pathways. These routes are then rigorously tested for security and efficiency. Once a safe route is identified, we use non-custodial smart contracts to activate liquidity without transferring asset custody.

Which chains/wallets are supported first?

We're initially supporting Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Polygon networks, with more chains being added quarterly. For wallets, we support all major non-custodial options including MetaMask, Ledger, Trezor, and WalletConnect-compatible wallets.

What's your business model?

We charge a small percentage fee on successfully activated liquidity routes. There are no hidden fees or upfront costs. Our free tier allows developers to experiment with our API, while professional and enterprise plans offer higher limits and additional features.

Security & audits?

Security is our top priority. All our smart contracts undergo rigorous auditing by leading security firms. We also have a bug bounty program and maintain comprehensive insurance coverage. Our architecture is designed with multiple layers of security and failsafes.

Compliance considerations?

We've built compliance into our core architecture. Our system includes built-in AML/KYC checks where required, travel rule compliance, and works closely with regulators to ensure our technology meets all legal requirements. We prioritize privacy while maintaining regulatory compliance.
