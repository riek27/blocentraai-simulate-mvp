Who We Are

Building South Sudan's Future Since Our Founding

Dhab Construction is a premier construction and real estate company based in Juba, South Sudan. With a rich heritage spanning decades and over 120 years of combined industry experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted leader in the construction sector across East Africa.

Our team comprises highly skilled engineers, architects, project managers, and construction specialists who bring unparalleled expertise to every project. We combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to deliver exceptional results that stand the test of time.

From modest residential buildings to large-scale infrastructure projects, our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation has earned us a reputation for excellence and reliability. We pride ourselves on our ability to understand client needs and translate them into functional, beautiful, and sustainable structures that enhance communities and drive economic growth.
